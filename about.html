About Us

“Hello! We are ‘Code for Change’, a group of high school students interested in computer science and design. As a part of our student-led initiative we want to support small businesses by creating and managing their websites and social media pages. During this process, we will work with you to design, develop and update the online presence of your business. We will charge a minimal fees, and all proceeds earned from this initiative will go towards promoting better digital literacy and educational technology for underprivileged youth. If any individual or small business is interested in our services, please contact us at codeforchange70@gmail.com, and we will be happy to work with you.”
